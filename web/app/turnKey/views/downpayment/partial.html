Down Payment Assistance

A home buyer will be eligible for the program if the applicant / household is a homebuyer who will occupy the property as his/her principal residence during the affordability period. The homebuyer must be low-to-moderate income, defined for the purpose of this program as having a gross annual income that does not exceed 80% of the City's median income adjusted for family size, demonstrate the ability to obtain a mortgage loan and sustain ownership over the period of affordability.
